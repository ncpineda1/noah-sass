Bios

Harvey Dale

Harvey Dale plays the guitar and is the vocalist. He graduated from the University of Texas at Austin in 2010 with a B.A. in psychology. In his free time, he likes to go to car shows and go to the casino.

Louis Cement

Louis Cement plays the bass guitar. He graduated from Texas A&M University in 2012 with a B.S. in poultry science. In his free time, he likes to play badminton and try out new recipes to cook.

Mike Cement

Mike Cement plays the drums. He graduated from Texas Tech University in 2011 with a B.A. in political science. In his free time, he likes to wakeboard and visit rodeos.
